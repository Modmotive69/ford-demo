Yakima® Rack Mounted Kayak Carrier w/o Lock

About This Item +

Adventure more with this Yakima kayak carrier allowing you to carry two boats in a vertical position that folds down when not in use!

  • Fully assembled out of the box, tool-free installation
  • Fits Yakima or O.E. crossbars
  • Adjust the upright position with the easy-to-use integrated cam lever
  • Folds flat when not in use
  • Includes Heavy-Duty Straps and Bow/Stern Tie-Downs
  • Supports one boat up to 80 lb or two boats with combined weight of 110 lbs or less
  • Minimum crossbar spreads of 24 inch or more
  • Not compatible with Bronco Raptor models
