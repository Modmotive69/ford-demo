About This Item

The Yakima Telescoping Bed Side Bars provide a versatile mounting platform for your truck bed. Adjusting from 36" to 64", they fit a wide range of Ford truck beds. Made from extruded aluminum with a matte black anodized finish, these bars are built to last. T-Slot compatible for easy accessory mounting. Sold as a set of 2.

  • Material: Extruded aluminum
  • Finish: Matte black anodized
  • Load rating: 300 lbs (distributed)
  • Mounting: T-Slot compatible
  • Weight: 18 lbs per pair
  • Includes: 2 bars, mounting hardware, instructions
